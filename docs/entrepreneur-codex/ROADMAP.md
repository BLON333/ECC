# Entrepreneur Codex Roadmap

This sequence is planning context, not authorization to begin a slice. Current
authorization and the recommended next slice are recorded in
[Current State](CURRENT.md). Accepted decisions and proposed directions are
recorded in [Decisions](DECISIONS.md). The normative lightweight-profile
design is the [ECC Lite Profile Contract](ECC_LITE_PROFILE.md).

1. **[Foundation and repository memory](NORTH_STAR.md)** — Establish the purpose, two-lane boundary, fresh-start direction, and durable repository-memory rules.
2. **[Operating contracts and active Insurance Desk state](PROJECT_SESSION_TOPOLOGY.md#document-responsibilities)** — Governed by the [Codex Project and Session Topology](PROJECT_SESSION_TOPOLOGY.md), [Tool and Connection Maintenance Contract](CONNECTION_MAINTENANCE.md), and [Insurance Knowledge Architecture](INSURANCE_KNOWLEDGE_ARCHITECTURE.md). The active private Insurance Desk supports operator-designated assist-only work outside Git while local Obsidian and Project configuration stabilization remains pending.
3. **[Lightweight Entrepreneur ECC profile](ECC_LITE_PROFILE.md)** —
   Stage 3A.2 is the completed documentation design. The later
   [Stage 3B implementation](ECC_LITE_PROFILE.md#stage-3b-implementation-boundary)
   is the recommended next repository slice and requires separate
   authorization; it is not authorized, active, or started.
4. **[Synthetic and sanitized insurance-assistance software pilot](INSURANCE_KNOWLEDGE_ARCHITECTURE.md#software-development-fixture-standard)** — Prove insurance-assistance software with permitted fixtures in Git. This fixture restriction does not prohibit operator-designated private assist-only use of real working material inside the active Insurance Desk.
5. **[Simple Software Studio starter](NORTH_STAR.md)** — Prove the separate product-building lane with one understandable starter and no shared product runtime.
6. **[Approved connections and broader operating controls](INSURANCE_KNOWLEDGE_ARCHITECTURE.md#proposed-future-work-email-architecture)** — Require the [Operator Contract](OPERATOR_CONTRACT.md#operator-approval-required) and [connection-maintenance contract](CONNECTION_MAINTENANCE.md) before Work IQ enablement, Microsoft-side components, connectors, cloud synchronization, an automated Microsoft 365 queue or file bridge, or an externally consequential action.
7. **[Evidence-based expansion or consolidation](ECC_LITE_PROFILE.md#human-governed-harness-improvement)** — Expand, reuse, or retire components only from material evidence and an accepted decision recorded in [Decisions](DECISIONS.md).

Roadmap placement never authorizes a slice. Stage 4's fixture-only restriction applies to testing and developing insurance-assistance software in Git, not to operator-designated private assist-only use of the active Insurance Desk. Stage 6 remains the separate gate for connectors, cloud synchronization, broader operating controls, and autonomous or externally consequential actions.
